Chocolate Covered Strawberries Recipe

Looking for one of the easiest and inexpensive desserts to serve? Make chocolate covered strawberries and enjoy a luxury confection that will be bound to impress your party!

What do you need?

  • 10 ounce package Ghirardelli Baking Chips Bittersweet, semi-sweet, or milk chocolate. Buy Ghirardelli Baking Chips!
  • 2 pounds fresh strawberries stems attached
  • Optional toppings: Chopped Nuts (peanuts, pistachios, pecans, etc.), Toasted coconut, Sprinkles, Mini chocolate chips (any flavor),Graham cracker crumbs, Crushed Oreo cookies, Melted white chocolate, Crushed candy canes

How do you do it?

  1. First, wash your strawberries! Make sure to dry them very well or else your chocolate will not stick properly.
  2. If you're using toppings, add a handful of your choice on a plate.
  3. Line a sheet pan with wax or parchment paper for your strawberries.
  4. Microwave your melting chocolate in a small bowl, removing and stirring every 30 seconds to ensure you won't burn the chocolate. Continue until chocolate is melted and smooth!
  5. Taking your washed and dried strawberry by the stem, dip into the melted chocolate. Lift and hover the strawberry to allow the excess chocolate to drip back into the bowl. This ensures an even coating of chocolate for each strawberry! Dip your strawberry into your desired toppings, then place onto your pan. Repeat with the rest of the strawberries.
  6. Finally, enjoy your delicious chocolate covered strawberries!

Enjoy your strawberries with the people you love for Valentine's Day, Birthdays, or any holiday for a fun and easy treat!
