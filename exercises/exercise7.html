Microsoft Word Manual: Microsoft Word for Marketers: A Time-Saving Guide

Microsoft Word for Marketers: A Time-Saving Guide is a comprehensive software documentation manual designed for marketing professionals and students. As my first long-term technical writing project, this guide breaks down essential Microsoft Word 2024 for Mac features, focusing on efficiency, organization, and document design. This project strengthened my ability to describe complex software functions into clear, actionable steps, demonstrating skills in technical communication, content structuring, and user-focused documentation. I also learned the process of user research by interviewing Subject Matter Experts(SME’s) and usability testing by conducting usability tests. This guide highlights my growth in problem-solving, instructional design, and creating an accessible resource that enhances productivity for a target audience.

Grant Proposal: Literacy for Leaders: An initiative to empower South Sudanese women through literacy and leadership development

This is a grant proposal for the Malala Fund advocating for an 8-week literacy and leadership program for South Sudanese women. This project involved deep research into grant writing, fundraising materials, and statistical analysis, while also integrating three forms of AI to enhance content development. Through this work, I honed my skills in persuasive writing, financial research, and strategic planning. Massachusetts Daily Collegian article: Women need to be prioritized in healthcare research

This article, published in the Massachusetts Daily Collegian, examines the systemic gender bias in healthcare and the critical need for women-specific medical research. I explored the historical exclusion of women from clinical trials, the consequences of misdiagnoses, and the disparities in research funding. Through investigative analysis and advocacy-driven writing, this piece highlights my ability to engage with complex social issues, synthesize research, and drive conversations on healthcare equity.
